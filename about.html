About Me

Info...

I am Jamil Ford, a skilled camera operator and multimedia director based in Lexington, Kentucky, with over three years of experience in video production. I excel in coordinating and executing video and photo shoots, ensuring high-quality results and effective teamwork. I have directed and edited the short film "NightWalker," worked as a Lighting and Grip/Boom Operator for "BCTC Profiles in Resiliency," and operated cameras for "The Covid Monologues" and "The Next Moment." My expertise extends to Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, Davinci Resolve, and various camera systems, including mirrorless and cinema cameras.Currently, I direct and edit social media content for local musicians, enhancing their online presence. My dedication has earned me a Bronze Telly Award for "The Covid Monologues." I am an active member of the Photography Club at the University of Kentucky, continuously honing my craft and contributing creatively to the field of video production.
